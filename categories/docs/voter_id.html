Voter ID Services

What is a Voter ID?

A Voter ID card is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India to citizens eligible to vote. It acts as both an identity proof and a voting right.

Why It's Important

  • Required for voting in elections
  • Accepted as valid ID proof
  • Prevents electoral fraud
  • Helps in government schemes and verification
