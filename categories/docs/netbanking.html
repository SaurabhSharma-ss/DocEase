Net Banking Registration

What is Net Banking?

Net Banking is an online platform provided by banks that allows customers to perform financial transactions over the internet without visiting a branch.

Benefits of Net Banking

  • 24/7 access to your bank account
  • Funds transfer, bill payments, and investments
  • Request cheque books and account statements
  • Secure and convenient banking from home

How to Register

1

Visit Official Site

Go to your bank’s official website and click on ‘Register for Net Banking’.

2

Provide Account Details

Enter your customer ID, account number, and registered mobile number.

3

Verify OTP

Authenticate using OTP sent to your registered mobile number.

4

Create Credentials

Set up your username and password to access your online account.
