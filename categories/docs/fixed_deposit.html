What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a financial instrument provided by banks and NBFCs that offers investors a higher rate of interest than a regular savings account, until the given maturity date.

Benefits of a Fixed Deposit

  • Higher interest rates than savings accounts
  • Low risk and guaranteed returns
  • Flexible tenure from 7 days to 10 years
  • Loan facility against FD
  • Helps in disciplined saving
