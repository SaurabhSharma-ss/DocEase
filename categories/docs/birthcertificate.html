Birth Certificate Services

What is a Birth Certificate?

A birth certificate is an official document that records a person's birth details including date, time, and place of birth, and parentage.

Importance of Birth Certificate

  • Proof of age and identity
  • Required for school admission
  • Needed for passport and legal documents

Application Process

1

Register Birth

Visit your local municipal authority or register online within 21 days of birth.

2

Submit Required Documents

Hospital discharge slip, ID of parents, and address proof required.

3

Download Certificate

Download digitally signed certificate from the official website.
