Gas Subsidy Linking

What is Gas Subsidy Linking?

Link your Aadhaar with LPG connection to avail government gas subsidy directly in your bank.

Why Do You Need Gas Subsidy Linking?

  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Prevents misuse of subsidy
  • Ensures transparency
