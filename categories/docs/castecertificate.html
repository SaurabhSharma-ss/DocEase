Caste Certificate Services

What is a Caste Certificate?

A caste certificate is an official document issued to certify a person belongs to a particular caste, especially if they are part of SC, ST, or OBC categories.

Why It’s Required

  • Availing reservations in education and jobs
  • Access to government benefits
  • Required for competitive exams

Application Process

1

Go to State Portal

Access the state’s official e-district services portal.

2

Submit Application

Provide caste details, income certificate, and proof of residence.

3

Download Certificate

Once approved, download your caste certificate online.
