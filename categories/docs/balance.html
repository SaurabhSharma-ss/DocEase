Balance Enquiry & Mini Statement

What is Balance Enquiry?

Balance enquiry allows you to check the current available balance in your bank account. You can also get a mini statement showing the last few transactions.

Ways to Check

  • Net banking or mobile banking
  • SMS banking
  • ATM machines
  • Bank branch visit
  • Phone call to toll-free number
