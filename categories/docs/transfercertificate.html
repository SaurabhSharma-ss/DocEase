Transfer Certificate (TC)

What is Transfer Certificate (TC)?

A Transfer Certificate is issued by an educational institution when a student leaves the school or college.

Why Do You Need Transfer Certificate (TC)?

  • Required for admission to a new institution
  • Indicates academic performance and behavior
  • Certifies discontinuation from the previous school
