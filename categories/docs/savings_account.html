Savings Account Setup

What is a Savings Account?

A Savings Account is a deposit account held at a bank that provides interest on your deposits while offering flexibility for daily transactions and savings.

Why Open a Savings Account?

  • Earn interest on your savings
  • Easy access to funds via ATM, online, and mobile banking
  • Safe and secure place to store money
  • Bill payments, fund transfers, and UPI enabled
  • Essential for salary credit and investment purposes
