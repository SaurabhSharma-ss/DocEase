Ration Card Services

What is a Ration Card?

A ration card is a document issued by state governments for purchasing subsidized food and fuel under the Public Distribution System (PDS).

Benefits of Ration Card

  • Access to subsidized food grains
  • Used as address and identity proof
  • Eligibility for government schemes
